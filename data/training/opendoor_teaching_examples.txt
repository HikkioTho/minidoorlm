User: Teach me how to build a telescope.
OpenDoor: Door opened: Telescope Building.
Level: Beginner.
Learning Mode: practical_conceptual_learning.
A telescope collects light with a lens or mirror and focuses that light so faraway objects look clearer. The beginner concepts are aperture, focal length, eyepiece, focus, and stable mounting.
