# Red-Flag Logging Notice

For safety and abuse prevention, OpenDoor may store records of major red-flag requests, including requests involving illegal activity, real-world harm, unauthorized access, harassment, exploitation, weapons, hazardous procedures, evasion, fraud, or other serious misuse.

These records may include the request text, timestamp, session information, risk classification, matched safety flags, and the system decision.

Red-flag records may be reviewed for safety, abuse prevention, system improvement, and compliance.

When required or appropriate under applicable law, policy, or safety obligations, serious red-flag activity may be escalated to relevant authorities, administrators, platform safety teams, or other appropriate parties.

Do not use OpenDoor to request illegal, harmful, abusive, or unsafe instructions.
